Overview of Professor Agyei's Achievements and Experience

Professor Agyei is a leading expert and research practitioner in ICT education in Ghana and across Africa.

Key Highlights

  • Ranked as the leading EdTech researcher in Ghana in the EdTech Hub's 2021 country-level research review.
  • Won the Best Paper Award from the Society for Information Technology and Teacher Education (SITE) at the 25th Annual International conference, in Jacksonville, USA in 2014.

Professional Experience

  • More than 12 years of professional experience in managing projects relating to ICT Education.
  • Served in different capacities in several international collaborations and consultancy projects.
  • Mostly served as the Lead Consultant in Monitoring and Evaluation projects, Impact projects, ICT-related projects, and Audit Reviews funded by Development Agencies such as:
    • USAID
    • Microsoft
    • British Council
    • UNESCO
    • World Bank
    • Ministry of Education, Ghana
