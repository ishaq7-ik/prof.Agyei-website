Profile

Prof. Agyei is one of a leading expert in ICT in Education with a PhD degree in Curriculum Design & Instructional Technology from University of Twente, the Netherlands and a Master’s degree in Applied Mathematics from the University of Kaisersalutern, Germany. He obtained his first degree in Mathematics Education, from the University of Cape Coast, Ghana. He has worked with international bodies such as UNESCO, USAID, and Microsoft on various impactful projects.
